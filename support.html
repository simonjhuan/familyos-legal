Common questions

  • How do I delete my data? Open the app → Settings → Delete Account. This removes your data from the device and, if cloud sync was on, from our servers. You can also email us to request deletion.
  • Does Family Pulse track my location? No. Location is only ever shared if you explicitly trigger SOS, and only for that moment. There is no background or continuous location tracking.
  • How do I cancel Family OS Premium? Manage or cancel your subscription in your device's Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions (iOS) or Play Store → Subscriptions (Android).
